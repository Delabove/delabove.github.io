About Me

After spending 16 years in a career that I happened to be good at, I stumbled upon one that I love.
For years, I was responsible for the the way other people felt about themselves when they looked into the mirror, all the while desiring something more meaningful. I didn't know what that looked like until I experienced the magic of composing a string of characters that allowed me to gather information about a user to create better online experiences.
I've often asked myself, "What skills can a hairstylist posses that is beneficial as a Software Developer?" During the bootcamp journey, I found that answer. My technical background allows me to visualize a project from a designer's perspective. My extensive study into 3 dimensional design and the Law Of Color lends itself useful when working with Graphic and UX designers. My experience with diagrams to pre-plan client services has given me the necessary tools for project planning. After 16 years of perfecting client consultations, I understand that asking the right questions is equally important as listening with the intent to understand. Most importantly, my professional background has helped me identify the core values I get to carry into this next chapter:

Self motivation, the willingness to be a lifelong learner, and an extraordinary work ethic.
