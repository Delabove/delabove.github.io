About Me

I am someone who thrives when a goal is presented to me. When I first toyed with the idea of becoming a Software Developer, I experienced internal push back. On paper, I am a hairstylist and salon owner. Hairstylists and a Software Developers, I thought, live on opposite sides of the career spectrum. It was difficult visualizing what value a company would potentially see in my professional background. The closest thing I had come to a position in technology was social media marketing and a small exposure to code and graphic design. However, that exposure peaked my curiosity enough to make me consider a career change.
It wasn't long after enrolling in Codeup when I realized how my creative, abstract thinking and eye for visual design would be an incredible asset for a developer. My background in haircutting allows me to see things from a designers point of view. My eyes are trained to see symmetry, shape, balance, and proportion. I understand the color wheel from a scientific perspective.
This learning journey has been the most fulfilling experience! The thing I have fallen in love with most about my new career is that it challenges me creatively AND intellectually! I've never felt more "at home" in my career than I do when I am coding!
